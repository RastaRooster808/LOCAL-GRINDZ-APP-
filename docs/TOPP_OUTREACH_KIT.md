# TOPP · Ohana Bloom — Florist & Hotel Outreach Kit

Wholesale outreach kit for TOPP's weekly Puna protea harvest. Strategy is
**premium and scarce, not volume**: ~50 stems/week supports only a handful of
standing accounts, so the scarcity *is* the pitch. Approach one account at a
time; let the first "yes" define capacity before pitching the next.

## Operating numbers (source of truth)

- **Yield:** ~50 premium stems / week
- **Cut & delivery:** Friday morning, same-day
- **Delivery area:** Islandwide; Hilo & Puna core, Volcano & Kona by arrangement
- **Lead time:** one week to open a standing account
- **Origin:** Puna a‘a lava, ~800–1,200 ft elevation
- **Species:** _Protea cynaroides_ (King), _Leucospermum_ (Pincushion),
  _Leucadendron_, seasonal island botanicals
- **Honest revenue ceiling:** ~$1,000–1,400/mo from flowers at this yield

## Tier 2 — Hilo farm-to-table & upscale restaurants

These already sell "local, single-origin" to their diners, so the story writes
itself — a Puna-grown protea at the host stand or on the bar is on-brand before
you say a word. Restaurant pitch differs from hotels: the flower isn't lobby
décor, it's a signal to the diner that *everything here is sourced this way*.
Fit is The Lobby (host stand / bar statement piece) or, for the small intimate
rooms, The Counter at the pass.
