**Benefits to disclose at purchase:**
- Early access to new TOPP botanical prints before public release
- Monthly member newsletter (Puna grower updates, new print previews)
- 10% discount on all digital print purchases
- Name listed in the TOPP Community Archive acknowledgements page (opt-in)

**Limitations (must be disclosed on product page):**
- Digital benefits only — no physical goods included in base tier
- Discount applies to digital prints; does not apply to Ohana Bloom wholesale orders

**Shopify product setup notes:**
1. Create product in Shopify Admin with type "Membership"

4. Attach plan to both variants with appropriate billing frequencies
5. Add product to "TOPP Memberships" collection
6. Add a "Membership Terms" section to the product page (see Required Disclosures below)

**Required disclosures on product page:**
- "This is a recurring subscription. You will be billed [monthly/annually] until you cancel."
- "Cancel anytime from your account dashboard."
- "Digital benefits are delivered via email. Allow 24 hours for initial setup."
- "This membership does not include physical product delivery."

**Cancellation note:** Customer can cancel via Shopify account portal or by emailing the store. No minimum term. Refunds at store discretion for unused partial periods.

**Subscription app requirement:** Shopify Subscriptions app (already installed). Create new selling plan group "TOPP Supporter" — do not attach this to the existing "Botanicals" plan.
